SageMath and CoCalc resources

As we go through the unit we shall make use of the SageMath software system to carry out investigations and calculations with the integers and groups. At the end of each chapter of the textbook there is a guided tutorial on relevant aspects of Sage.

SageMath is a free open-source mathematics software system licensed under the GPL. It builds on top of many existing open-source packages: NumPy, SciPy, matplotlib, Sympy, Maxima, GAP, FLINT, R and many more, accessing their combined power through a common, Python-based language.

CoCalc is an online computing service that provides access to a large collection of open-source mathematical software, including Sage.

Below you will find links to the CoCalc site and two books on Sage that are free to download. The first book has lots of good introductory material on Sage while the second takes things further.
