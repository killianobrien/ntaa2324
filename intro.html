Welcome to the Calculus unit. In this unit we shall be studying the concepts of limiting values of functions and the derivative and integral of functions of a single variable. We shall also look at the concepts of sequences and series of real numbers. 

In this unit you shall see an increased level of mathematical rigour compared to your pre-university studies. We shall be concerned with proving the various properties of convergence, differentiation and integration that we use.

The main resource we shall use for this unit is a selection of relevant chapters from the excellent free open-source textbook APEX Calculus by Gregory Hartman, Sean Fitzpatrick (Editor), Alex Jordan (Editor), Carly Vollet (Editor). This book has a wealth of explanations, examples and exercises to help you learn about these topics. It doesn't have as many proofs as we need -- so we shall supply any necessary extra proofs in the lectures. The book contains topics that go beyond our Calculus unit so don't be daunted by the size of it. But you should find these extra chapters interesting reading also. A link to the HTML version of this book is given below. This version will work really well on any size of device.
